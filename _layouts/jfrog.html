Unlock the Full Snyk Database

A basic subset of Snyk’s vulnerability database (“Snyk Basic”) comes pre-installed into JFrog Xray out of the box, with no add-ons or API keys required to activate it.

For even deeper security coverage inside JFrog Xray you can upgrade to the full “Snyk Premium” Vulnerability Database by becoming a Snyk Enterprise or Snyk Pro customer. To inquire about becoming a Snyk Enterprise or Snyk Pro customer and unlocking the full “Snyk Premium” database inside JFrog Xray please contact contact@snyk.io. For existing Enterprise or Pro customers, a quick guide on how to upgrade to Snyk Premium is available here: How to Upgrade To Snyk Premium.”
